Democracy First! Fridays

A proposition for a series of community events in Missoula

Stand Up for Democracy. Build Community. Take Action.

What is Democracy First! Fridays?

Like the popular First Friday Art Walks that take place on the first Friday of each month, Democracy First! Fridays are a network of popup community events throughout Downtown Missoula with the aim at celebrating, protecting, and expanding our democratic values. This unique time in the history of our country requires a united pushback against the ongoing dismantling of our democratic institutions. There is no other time than now for communities to engage in open discussions, artistic expression, grassroots organizing, cooperation, education, and mutual aid.

A united community can make a difference.

Get Involved

There are many ways to contribute to Democracy First! Fridays:

  • Host an Event – Do you have a special skill or knowledge that you can share with others? Create an inclusive experience that fosters dialogue, learning, and action.
  • Join an Event – Participate in Democracy First! events. Find a local gathering and connect with others who care about democracy.
  • Volunteer – Support event organizers, spread the word, or help with logistics.
  • Artists & Creatives – Use your talents to create performances, murals, poetry readings, or exhibits that educate and inspire civic engagement.
  • Community Organizers – Plan discussions, protests, or action-oriented workshops that mobilize people.
  • Businesses & Local Shops – Host events in your space, sponsor activities, or donate resources to community initiatives.
  • Nonprofits & Advocacy Groups – Partner with us to raise awareness, provide educational materials, or engage volunteers in meaningful action.

What You Can Do

All Democracy First! Fridays are inclusive events where everyone feels welcome and empowered. Here are just a few sample ways to bring the campaign to life in your community:

  • Public Forums – Discuss topics like voting rights, free speech, or local policies.
  • Art Exhibits – Showcase diverse artists whose work champions democracy and equality.
  • Film Screenings – Share powerful stories about social justice and civic action.
  • Voter Registration Drives – Ensure that everyone, including marginalized voices, has access to voting.
  • Live Music – Celebrate free expression through music and performance.
  • Hands-On Workshops – Host workshops around topics of civic education for the general public that are inclusive and open to all.
  • Community Dialogues – Encourage meaningful, respectful conversations that include people from different backgrounds and perspectives.
  • Book Readings or Storytelling Events – Feature works from voices who are adamant about protecting our democracy and civil rights.
  • Community Dinners or Potlucks – Bring people together over food and meaningful conversations.
  • Street Performance or Plays – Use theater to engage the public in democratic themes.
  • Community Service Projects – Support local needs while reinforcing values of equity, access, and participation.
  • Open Mic Nights – Give people a space to share their thoughts, music, poetry, or speeches.
  • Peaceful Demonstrations or Rallies – Advocate for causes that matter to your community.

Why It Matters

Democracy is more than elections—it’s about active participation, free expression, and standing up for our civil rights. By taking part in Democracy First! Fridays, you’re not just attending an event—you’re helping to create a society where everyone is seen, heard, and valued.

If all that sounds good to you, let’s get started.
