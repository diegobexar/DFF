Democracy First Fridays

Stand Up for Democracy. Build Community. Take Action.

Every Friday, we come together to celebrate, protect, and expand democratic values. Whether through open discussions, artistic expression, or grassroots organizing, you can make a difference. Our events are inclusive to all—welcoming every person, regardless of age, race, gender, ethnicity, sexual orientation, nationality, legal status, or abilities.

Get Involved

  • Host an Event: Create inclusive experiences that spark dialogue.
  • Join an Event: Attend local gatherings that inspire action.
  • Volunteer: Assist with organizing or spreading the word.
  • Artists & Creatives: Share your voice through visual and performance art.
  • Community Organizers: Lead discussions, protests, and workshops.
  • Businesses & Nonprofits: Sponsor, host, or support local events.

What You Can Do

All Democracy First Fridays events should be inclusive and welcoming. Here are some event ideas:

  • Host a public forum or town hall
  • Organize a voter registration drive
  • Create an art exhibit or mural
  • Screen a documentary on civil rights
  • Hold a community dinner or potluck
  • Lead a civic education workshop
  • Set up an open mic night
  • Facilitate a storytelling or book reading event
  • Plan a community service project
  • Host live music or theater performance
  • Organize a peaceful demonstration
  • Create space for cross-cultural dialogue

Why It Matters

Democracy is about more than just elections. It’s about active participation, free expression, and civil rights. By joining Democracy First Fridays, you help shape a society where everyone is valued and heard.
