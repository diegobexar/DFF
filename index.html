What is Democracy First! Fridays?

Like the popular First Friday Art Walks that take place on the first Friday of each month, Democracy First! Fridays are a network of popup community events throughout Downtown Missoula that happen every Friday and are aimed at celebrating, protecting, and expanding our democratic values.

This unique time in the history of our country requires a united pushback against the ongoing dismantling of our democratic institutions. There is no other time than now for communities to engage in open discussions, artistic expression, grassroots organizing, cooperation, education, and mutual aid.

A united community can make a difference.

What You Can Do

Here are just a few ideas to bring Democracy First! Fridays to life in Missoula:

Public Forums

Discuss topics like voting rights, free speech, or local policies.

Art Exhibits

Showcase diverse artists whose work champions democracy and equality.

Film Screenings

Share powerful stories about social justice and civic action.

Voter Registration Drives

Ensure that everyone, including marginalized voices, has access to voting.

Live Music

Celebrate free expression through music and performance.

Hands-On Workshops

Host workshops around topics of civic education for the general public that are inclusive and open to all.

Community Dialogues

Encourage meaningful, respectful conversations that include people from different backgrounds and perspectives.

Book Readings or Storytelling Events

Feature works from voices who are adamant about protecting our democracy and civil rights.

Community Dinners or Potlucks

Bring people together over food and meaningful conversations.

Street Performance or Plays

Use theater to engage the public in democratic themes.

Community Service Projects

Support local needs while reinforcing values of equity, access, and participation.

Open Mic Nights

Give people a space to share their thoughts, music, poetry, or speeches.

Why It Matters

Democracy is more than elections—it’s about active participation, free expression, and standing up for our civil rights. By taking part in Democracy First! Fridays, you’re not just attending an event—you’re helping to create a society where everyone is seen, heard, and valued.

If all that sounds good to you, let’s get started.
